This Weeks Events

Please note that specific event times and details may vary, so always a good idea to check with the coffee shop directly or visit their website for the most up-to-date information. Additionally, some events might require reservations or have limited seating, so it's a idea to plan ahead if you're interested in attending a particular event.

  • Morning Yoga Class: Start your week with a relaxing yoga session. (8:00 AM - 9:00 AM)
  • Live Acoustic Music: Enjoy acoustic performances by local artists. (6:30 PM - 8:30 PM)

Friday

  • Live Jazz Band: Relax and unwind with the smooth tunes of a live jazz band. (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)

Saturday

  • Brunch Live Brunch: Delight in a delicious brunch menu with options like avocado toast, pancakes, and freshly brewed coffee. Enjoy live music performances by local musicians while you dine. (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
