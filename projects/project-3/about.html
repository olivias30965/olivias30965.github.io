Where it started

Inspired by their mutual adoration for this beloved beverage, they hatched a plan to open their very own coffee shop, where they struck up a conversation over their shared enthusiasm for single-origin beans. Their dream was to create a space where people could gather, connect, and forge new friendships over the finest brews.
