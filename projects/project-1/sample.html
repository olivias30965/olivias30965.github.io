ABOUT CENTRAL PA

Central Pennsylvania is home to many diverse environments and activities. From hiking to amusements parks to museums, Central PA has plenty of experiences to offer for everyone.

OUR TOP 6 TOURIST RECOMMENDATIONS FOR CENTRAL PA

Hershey Park

A renowned amusement park featuring exciting rides, live entertainment, and an opportunity to learn about chocolate-making at Hershey's Chocolate World.

Pennsylvania State Capitol

An impressive architectural gem in Harrisburg showcasing exquisite artwork, stunning interiors, and guided tours to learn about the state's government and history.

Railroad Museum of Pennsylvania

This museum boasts an extensive collection of vintage locomotives, rail cars, and exhibits, showcasing the rich history of railroading.

The Pennsylvania Grand Canyon

This natural wonder features stunning vistas, hiking trails, and opportunities for outdoor recreation.

Central Market York

The oldest continually operating farmers’ market in the U.S., offering a bustling atmosphere, local produce, crafts, and diverse food vendors.

Worlds End State Park

Known for its rugged beauty, with hiking trails, waterfalls, and breathtaking views of the Loyalsock Creek Gorge.
