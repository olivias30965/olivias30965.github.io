How to Make: Origami Lucky Paper Stars

Step 6: Shaping

• With the finished pentagon, there are two different ways to make the star shape:

1. Pinch each corner to puff the star up.

2. Push in the edges to puff the star up (this method is a bit easier, especially if you have longer nails).
